About Us

Welcome to The Additive Manufacturing Exchange, your gateway to the 21st Century Manufacturing Industry.

A next-generation marketplace

This next generation of manufacturing is based on Additive Manufacturing (AM) technology. AM technology is the same as 3D printing, which adds material to form an object rather than conventional manufacturing that cuts material away to shape an object.

As AM technology matures, it promises to change completely the manufacturing industry. Manufacturing will be faster, better and cheaper. AM also allows the manufacturing of components with higher performance than conventional means as well as the ability to produce new features and hollow components. If you want to reap the benefits of this global change in manufacturing, the Additive Manufacturing Exchange is your doorway.

The Exchange offers buyers of AM produced parts the means of connecting with and conducting business with additive manufacturers in a digital environment. The Exchange is the Digital Marketplace for the AM Industry. Whether you are an individual seeking to print a model, a metal toy or a novelty item or an industrial company seeking to procure a complex part through AM, the Exchange will connect you with AM manufacturers that have been evaluated for your specific needs.

The Exchange will analyze your requirement and provide you with a list of qualified providers and the tools to complete your transaction. Your transaction from selection through delivery and payment will be quickly completed using our digital marketplace.
