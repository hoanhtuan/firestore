About Company

The Additive Manufacturing Exchange (AMEX), LLC was organized in the Commonwealth of Virginia in 2014 to digitize business processes for the additive manufacturing industry. The Co-Owners and founders are Morgan Donaldson and Greg Orndorff. Morgan is a retired Naval Officer. Greg is a retired Air Force Officer.
