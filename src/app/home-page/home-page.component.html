Why Additive Manufacturing Exchange

Our Exchange offers buyers of Additive Manufacturing ("AM") produced parts the means of connecting with and conducting business with additive manufacturers in a digital environment. The Exchange is the Digital Marketplace for the AM Industry.

Whether you are an individual seeking to print a model, a metal toy or a novelty item or an industrial company seeking to procure a complex part through AM, the Exchange will connect you with AM manufacturers that have been evaluated for your specific needs.

The Exchange will analyze your requirement and provide you with a list of qualified providers and the tools to complete your transaction. Your transaction from selection through delivery and payment will be quickly completed using our digital marketplace.

Get Started

Buyers and sellers must first be registered as an individual or a company. Registration will establish a user’s account that will uniquely identify the individual or company to ensure accurate processing in the Exchange. Registration will also offer subscription options. In the early stages of the development of our various tools, customers can use the Exchange without a subscription.

Sellers of additive manufacturing capability enter, through their registration, their AM capabilities by industry sector, AM machine capability, material capability, software processing capability and final finishing capabilities.

Buyers of additive manufacturing capability enter, through their registration, their Name or Company Name, Address, POC and Credit Scores (individual) or D&B Paydex Score (D&B D-U-N-S Number Required) (Company).
